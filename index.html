About Us

WHO ARE WE ?

We have our own staff of professional cleaners who are
experienced in the industry, hired in the General Social Security
Regime in Malta so you do not have to worry about any
labor-related responsibility on the staff. In addition, our cleaning
services for the home, offices and businesses are covered with a
Civil Liability Insurance for your total peace of mind.

Our Services

WHAT WE ARE DOING ?

We offer the greatest flexibility in our services to meet the needs
of our customers. We follow the desired cleaning hours of your
offices, homes, and businesses at the time you need on a 24/7
availability. With the support of a personal advisor which we will
assign to you, you can set up an hourly service suitable for your
office, home, or business. You will only have to pay for the hours
you hire the service and thus avoid unpleasant surprises.
